Empayar: Hikayat Putera tanpa nama by Teme Abdullah

Rate:9.5/10

Reviews:"Empayar: Hikayat Putera Tanpa Nama" is Teme Abdullah's debut fiction novel and the first book in the Empayar series. It is a highly-rated work of dramatic, post-apocalyptic fantasy set in a reimagined historical Melayu sultanate. The Core Story The novel is set in the Kesultanan Melayu Aradhanapura, where the main setting is the Madrasah Taming Sari, an elite institution for training scholars and warriors. The story focuses on a prince who runs away from the palace and hides his true identity to study at the madrasah, believing his royal lineage is corrupted by black magic or dark practices. The prince, often referred to only by his training number, is on a quest for the true meaning of power, religion, and justice within a system that appears morally compromised.

In this book, the readers will get a deep thematic content because the central theme is about "religious knowledge is a weapon" it can be used for immense good or catastrophic evil. This book also have an unique world-building that feature a rare "classic-futuristic" and "religi-fantansy" setting, merging traditional Melayu sultanete imagery with elements of post-apocalypse and advanced learning institutions. It engaging and thought- provoking that many find the prose straightforward and easy to follow, yet the underlying message is deep and uses social commentary to criticize those who missue religion for personal gain.

Book 4

Will you stay by Norhafsah Hamid

Rate: 6/10

Reviews: Norhafsah Hamid's Will You Stay? is a heartwarming, faith-infused romance centered on Amy, a young, ambitious surgeon-in-training, and Rasyid, a kind lawyer and hafiz. The novel beautifully portrays the start of their relationship, focusing on mutual respect and shared faith (often dubbed "halal romance"). However, Amy's happiness is quickly tested by personal trials, forcing her to confront the limits of human love and the necessity of surrender to God. The book is rich with spiritual reminders and is a great read for those seeking a story that successfully blends contemporary love with deep Islamic reflection. While the writing is sometimes simple, its strong message about choosing faith and resilience in the face of heartbreak makes it an inspiring and emotionally resonant read. It's a reminder that true love should always lead one closer to the Divine.
